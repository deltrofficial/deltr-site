Deltr Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

What Deltr is

Deltr is a local first app for capturing before, progress, and after improvements.

Data stored in Deltr

Deltr stores content you create or select in the app, including:

  • Photos and videos you capture or select
  • Before, progress, and after descriptions
  • Profile information you enter
  • Local app, session, and reward metadata

Where your data is stored

Your Deltr data is stored locally on your device.

What Deltr does not do in v1

  • No account system
  • No backend server
  • No cloud sync
  • No third party advertising
  • No sale of personal data

Permissions

Deltr requests permissions needed for core app features:

  • Camera, to capture photos or videos
  • Microphone, to record audio with videos
  • Photo library, to select or save media

Your control

You control your Deltr data through the app and your device. You may delete content in the app, reset app data, or uninstall the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact deltr.io.official@gmail.com.
