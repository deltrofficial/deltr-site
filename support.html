Help topics

Camera permission issues

Check your device settings and make sure camera access is enabled for Deltr.

Microphone permission issues

Check your device settings and make sure microphone access is enabled for Deltr.

Photo library access

Check your device settings and make sure photo library access is enabled for Deltr.

Deleting deltas

Open the delta you want to remove and use the delete option in the app.

Resetting local data

You may reset local app data from the app settings, or uninstall Deltr from your device.
